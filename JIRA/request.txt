GPS/GNSS Update
GNSS Quality :
High-quality GNSS performance depends on three critical components :
GNSS chip
Oscillator
Antenna
Google provides tools and quality thresholds to evaluate and ensure GNSS performance on Android phones and Wear OS watches.
Analysis Tools :
GnssLogger App
Collects raw GNSS measurements.
gnss_analysis.ipynb (Colab)
Analyzes GNSS quality metrics.
Network connection required for :
                - Assistance data
                - SatPvt (required by gnss_analysis)
Quality Thresholds :
35 GNSS quality metrics with defined test thresholds.
For best DGNSS performance, devices should :
       - Support L1 + L5
       - Support ADR (carrier phase)
       - Pass all defined quality tests.
